"To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

"You have to be true to yourself or you will regret it." Wise words I once read from a long forgotten article, or perhaps from a glossy book from the self-help section, back when books and bookstores were ubiquitous. At least if you were lucky they were. People usually aren't true to themselves, even until the day they're swimming with the fishes. The lucky few might actually read the signs or listen to their gut and realize they're not living their own life, they're living someone else's. From there I decided, I wanted to join the world... I have a pure belief that whatever you strive for, you can accomplish. And so I decided I will learn to code, and be damn good at it.

I am hungry for a job that is creative, challenging, and positive. Being able to contribute to something good, as well as, learn and grow on the job, is an ideal career path for me.

I believe our personal histories are an important part of who we are now. I was raised as a Japanese American, and that is who I am now, and at my core, that is who I will always be.
