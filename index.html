About

"To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Follow your passion. Words I try to live by since my grandma's passing. She said these words to me when she came up to see me graduate from UC Santa Cruz. By then the cancer that was in remission for over a decade had already invited itself back in and this time for good. She was the light of our family, an ideal to strive for. Even now, I struggle to stay true to her words, but even at my darkest, those words pull me back up above dark waters. Her kindness, her strength, give me courage to follow my dreams no matter the naysayers, no matter the doubt. As for my passion, it's an ever evolving passion but it rests on a fundamental purpose: service. The only thing that truly makes my soul sing is helping people, to be of service, to be useful. It may not be a glamorous passion, hell, it may not even be a useful passion for some, but with passion you can move mountains. So, I follow my passion.
